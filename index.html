About Us

Where Passion Meets the Deep Blue

IMAYA AQUATICS is Bangalore's premium destination for aquarium enthusiasts. We are not just a fish shop — we are a curated experience for those who love the art of aquascaping and the joy of keeping exotic aquatic life.

From rare imported species to professional-grade tanks and aquascaping equipment, every product in our collection is handpicked for quality, health, and beauty.

  • Live imported exotic fish from trusted breeders
  • Expert aquascaping advice and setup guidance
  • Premium filtration, lighting & CO₂ systems
  • Rare plants, substrate & hardscape materials
  • Free consultation for new tank setups
