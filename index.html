Our Story

Crafting Living
Underwater Worlds

IMAYA AQUATICS is Bengaluru's premier destination for rare and exotic ornamental fish. We curate exceptional aquatic life — from luminous Bettas and electric Discus to rare Arowanas and breathtaking Flowerhorn Cichlids — for collectors and enthusiasts who demand the extraordinary.

Every fish in our care is sourced from responsible breeders and maintained under ideal conditions. We believe an aquarium is not merely a tank — it is a living, breathing masterpiece that transforms any space into something transcendent.

Our team of experts guides you through species selection, aquarium setup, water chemistry, and long-term care — ensuring your aquatic world thrives for years to come.
